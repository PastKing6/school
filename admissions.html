Admission Process

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form and submit required documents, including previous academic records and recommendations.

2

Assessment

Students complete entrance assessments in English and Mathematics. International students may be required to take an English proficiency test.

3

Interview

Prospective students and parents meet with our admissions team for an interview to discuss educational goals and expectations.

4

Decision & Enrollment

Admission decisions are communicated within two weeks. Upon acceptance, families complete enrollment paperwork and pay deposits.

Admission Requirements

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Previous school records (2 years)
  • Teacher recommendations
  • Birth certificate or passport
  • Health records and immunizations
  • Recent photograph

Academic Requirements

  • Satisfactory academic performance
  • English proficiency (for non-native speakers)
  • Grade-appropriate mathematics skills
  • Positive behavioral record
  • Demonstrated learning motivation

Additional Requirements

  • Parent interview participation
  • Student interview
  • Entrance assessment completion
  • School visit (recommended)
  • Financial responsibility agreement
