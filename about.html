Our Story

Founded in 1995, Academia Linguarum Internationalis began with a vision to create an educational institution that combines academic excellence with global perspective. Our founders, a group of educators and diplomats, recognized the need for an educational approach that prepares students for an increasingly interconnected world.

Starting with just 45 students in a small building in central Warsaw, ALI has grown to become one of Poland's most respected international schools, now serving over 800 students from more than 40 countries.

Throughout our history, we have maintained a commitment to innovative teaching methods, cultural understanding, and personalized education that honors each student's unique potential.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape everything we do at Academia Linguarum Internationalis.

Our Mission

To provide an exceptional education that balances academic rigor with personal development, preparing students to be lifelong learners, critical thinkers, and responsible global citizens who contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as a center of educational excellence that nurtures culturally-aware, intellectually curious, and ethically responsible individuals who are empowered to shape a better world.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We pursue excellence in all aspects of education, maintaining high standards and encouraging each member of our community to achieve their personal best.

Integrity

We act with honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all interactions, fostering mutual respect and trust within our community.

Diversity

We celebrate cultural diversity and different perspectives, creating an inclusive environment where all members feel valued and respected.

Innovation

We embrace creative thinking and adapt to evolving educational approaches, preparing students for the challenges of a rapidly changing world.

Collaboration

We foster cooperative relationships among students, teachers, parents, and the wider community, recognizing that education is a shared responsibility.

Responsibility

We encourage social responsibility, environmental stewardship, and active citizenship, inspiring our students to make a positive difference in the world.
