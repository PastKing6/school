Program Overview

The Computer Science program at Academia Linguarum Internationalis provides students with a strong foundation in computational thinking, programming, and digital literacy. Our curriculum is designed to prepare students for university-level studies in computer science and related fields, as well as for careers in the rapidly evolving technology sector.

Through a combination of theoretical knowledge and practical application, students develop the skills needed to understand and create technology solutions for real-world problems. Our approach emphasizes both technical proficiency and ethical considerations in the digital age.

The program is available to students in our Middle Years Program (MYP) and Diploma Program (DP), with age-appropriate content and complexity at each level.

Key Program Features

Hands-on Coding

Students learn multiple programming languages including Python, Java, and JavaScript through practical, project-based assignments.

Computational Thinking

Develop problem-solving skills through algorithmic thinking, abstraction, pattern recognition, and decomposition.

Emerging Technologies

Explore cutting-edge fields including artificial intelligence, machine learning, and robotics through specialized modules.

Collaborative Projects

Work in teams to design, develop, and deploy software solutions, mirroring real-world development environments.

Digital Ethics

Understand the ethical implications of technology, including privacy, security, and social impact considerations.

Industry Connections

Benefit from guest lectures, field trips, and internship opportunities with leading technology companies in Poland and beyond.

Curriculum

Middle Years Program (Ages 11-16)

Foundational Skills

  • Introduction to computational thinking
  • Basic programming concepts with Scratch and Python
  • Web development fundamentals (HTML, CSS)
  • Digital literacy and online safety
  • Basic data structures and algorithms

Projects & Activities

  • Creating interactive games and animations
  • Building personal websites
  • Robotics club with LEGO Mindstorms
  • Participation in regional coding competitions
  • Digital media creation

Diploma Program (Ages 16-19)

Advanced Topics

  • Object-oriented programming with Java
  • Data structures and advanced algorithms
  • Database design and SQL
  • Computer systems and architecture
  • Software development lifecycle
  • Network principles and cybersecurity
  • Introduction to AI and machine learning

Projects & Activities

  • Capstone software development project
  • Mobile app development
  • Hackathons and coding competitions
  • Research projects in emerging technologies
  • Internship opportunities with tech companies
  • Mentorship program with industry professionals

University and Career Pathways

University Preparation

Our Computer Science program prepares students for admission to top universities worldwide. Graduates have been accepted to prestigious institutions including:

  • Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT)
  • Stanford University
  • ETH Zurich
  • University of Oxford
  • Technical University of Warsaw
  • Jagiellonian University
  • And many other leading institutions

Career Opportunities

The skills developed in our program prepare students for diverse career paths in the technology sector, including:

  • Software Development
  • Data Science and Analytics
  • Cybersecurity
  • Artificial Intelligence Research
  • Game Development
  • Web and Mobile App Development
  • IT Consulting
  • Technology Entrepreneurship
